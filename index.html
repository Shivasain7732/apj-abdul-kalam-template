A . P . J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (15 October 1931 - 27 july 2015) was an Indian actospace scientiest who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007.He was born and raised in Rameshwaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and acrospace engineering. He spent the next four decades as a sceientist and sceince administrator mainly at the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and Indian space Researc Orgranisation (ISRO) and was intimately involved in India's civilan space programme and military missile development effort. He thus came to be known as the Missile Man f India for his work on the dvelopment of balistc missile and laucj vehicle technology. he aslo played a pivotal organisational, technical and political role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998, the first since the original nuclear test by India in 1974.
